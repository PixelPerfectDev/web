Features
  • 🔎 Search for your personal depots and public ones.
  • 🖥 Keep track of pipelines and show jobs details.
  • 🛠 Trigger, abort and retry pipeline or job.
  • 📋 Display build log.
  • 💾 Download artifacts.
  • 📝 Manage project and group variable in one place for each depot.
